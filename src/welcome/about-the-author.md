### Hello!!

Credits for techniques from other researchers belong to the original authors (see references). Kudos to everyone who shares knowledge to improve internet security.

HackTricks is an educational wiki about **cyber-security** led by Carlos with hundreds of collaborators. It's a **huge collection of hacking tricks** updated by the community as much as possible. If you find something missing or outdated, please send a **Pull Request** to the [**Hacktricks GitHub**](https://github.com/hacktricks-wiki/hacktricks)!

HackTricks is also a wiki where **researchers share their latest findings**, so it's a great place to keep up to date with the latest hacking techniques.
