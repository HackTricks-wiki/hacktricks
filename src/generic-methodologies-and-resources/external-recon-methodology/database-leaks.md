## Data Breach Search Engines

- [greynoise](https://viz.greynoise.io/) - Soek vir IPs, Tags, CVEs, vpn, dns...
- [Dehashed](https://www.dehashed.com/) - Jy kan jou email soek as dit in sommige databasisse in 'n leak voorkom.
- [HaveIbeenPwned?](https://haveibeenpwned.com/) - Kontroleer of jou emailadres in 'n data breach voorkom
- [ScamSearch](https://scamsearch.io/) - Soek om telefoon, email of profiel te vind indien dit 'n scammer is.
- [Intelligence X](https://intelx.io/) - Intelligence X is 'n soekenjin en data-argief. Die soektog werk met selectors, bv. spesifieke soekterme soos emailadresse, domeine, URLs, IPs...
- [spycloud](https://portal.spycloud.com/endpoint/enriched-stats/[YOUR-MAIL]) - sit jou mail in YOUR-MAIL.
- [weleakinfo](https://weleakinfo.io/) - We Leak Info - Leaked Dehashed Databases, soek vir leaks.
- [breachdirectory](https://breachdirectory.org/) - Kontroleer of jou email of gebruikersnaam gekompromiteer is
- [leakcheck](https://leakcheck.io/) - Vind uit of jou credentials gekompromiteer is
- [Findemail.io](https://findemail.io/) - Vind emailadresse vir 'n gegewe maatskappy.
- [LibraryOfLeaks](https://search.libraryofleaks.org) - Soek vir leak-dokumente, maatskappye, mense, databases en meer
- [LeakRadar](https://leakradar.io/) - Onmiddellike soektog oor 2 B+ plain-text info-stealer credentials; email, domain, metadata queries, monitoring & API
- [InfoStealers](https://infostealers.info/en/info) - Indekseer darknet-exposed infostealer logs en maak dit deursoekbaar en bruikbaar vir security teams, investigators, navorsers en digital forensics professionals.
- [Leak-Lookup](https://leak-lookup.com/) - Data breach soekenjin met meer as 3 miljard rekords uit 3000+ databases.
- [BreachDirectory](https://breachdirectory.org/) - Kontroleer of jou credentials gekompromiteer is in data breaches.
- [Scylla.so](https://scylla.so/) - Community-driven database breach search engine.
- [Leaked.domains](https://leaked.domains/) - Soek vir domeingerelateerde leaked credentials en data.
- [WhiteIntel](https://whiteintel.io/) - Dark web data leak soekenjin vir threat intelligence.
- [PSBDMP](https://psbdmp.ws/) - Pastebin dump soek- en monitoringplatform.

## Tools to enumerate data leaks

- [Leaker](https://github.com/vflame6/leaker) - Passiewe leak-enumerasie CLI-instrument wat tegelyk oor 10 breach-databasisse soek.
