## Injini za Utafutaji za Uvujaji wa Data

- [greynoise](https://viz.greynoise.io/) - Tafuta kwa IPs, Tags, CVEs, vpn, dns...
- [Dehashed](https://www.dehashed.com/) - Unaweza kutafuta barua pepe yako ikiwa imeleak katika baadhi ya databases.
- [HaveIbeenPwned?](https://haveibeenpwned.com/) - Angalia kama anwani yako ya barua pepe ipo katika uvujaji wa data
- [ScamSearch](https://scamsearch.io/) - Tafuta ili upate namba ya simu, barua pepe au wasifu ikiwa ni mtapeli.
- [Intelligence X](https://intelx.io/) - Intelligence X ni search engine na data archive. Utafutaji unafanya kazi kwa selectors, yaani maneno maalum ya utafutaji kama anuani za barua pepe, domains, URLs, IPs...
- [spycloud](https://portal.spycloud.com/endpoint/enriched-stats/[YOUR-MAIL]) - weka barua pepe yako kwenye YOUR-MAIL.
- [weleakinfo](https://weleakinfo.io/) - We Leak Info - Leaked Dehashed Databases, search for leaks.
- [breachdirectory](https://breachdirectory.org/) - ANGALIA IKIWA BARUA PEPE YAKO AU JINA LA MTUMIAJI LILIATHIRIWA
- [leakcheck](https://leakcheck.io/) - Gundua kama kredenshiali zako zimeathiriwa
- [Findemail.io](https://findemail.io/) - Pata anwani za barua pepe za kampuni iliyotajwa.
- [LibraryOfLeaks](https://search.libraryofleaks.org) - Tafuta leak nyaraka, kampuni, watu, database na zaidi
- [LeakRadar](https://leakradar.io/) - Utafutaji wa papo hapo kwenye zaidi ya rekodi 2B+ za plain-text info-stealer credentials; email, domain, metadata queries, monitoring & API
- [InfoStealers](https://infostealers.info/en/info) - Inaindex logs za infostealer zilizofichuliwa kwenye darknet na kuzieka katika mfumo unaotafutika kwa timu za usalama, wachunguzi, watafiti, na wataalamu wa forensics za dijiti.
- [Leak-Lookup](https://leak-lookup.com/) - Search engine ya data breach yenye rekodi zaidi ya bilioni 3 kutoka databases 3000+.
- [BreachDirectory](https://breachdirectory.org/) - Angalia kama kredenshiali zako zimeruhusiwa/vimetoweka katika data breaches.
- [Scylla.so](https://scylla.so/) - Search engine ya database breach inayoendeshwa na jamii.
- [Leaked.domains](https://leaked.domains/) - Tafuta leaked credentials na data zinazohusiana na domain.
- [WhiteIntel](https://whiteintel.io/) - Dark web data leak search engine kwa ajili ya threat intelligence.
- [PSBDMP](https://psbdmp.ws/) - Jukwaa la utafutaji na ufuatiliaji wa Pastebin dump.

## Zana za kuorodhesha data leaks

- [Leaker](https://github.com/vflame6/leaker) - CLI tool ya passive leak enumeration inayotafuta kwa pamoja katika databases 10 za breach.
